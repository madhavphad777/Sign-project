Sign Language

Primarily used as a means of communication for the deaf or hard of hearing, there are 300 different forms of sign language around the world.

Sign Language Speakers

Just like spoken languages, there is more than one type of sign language. More than 300 different sign languages are actively used throughout the world.

Sign languages are as diverse and unique as spoken languages, with over 300 different sign languages actively used around the world. Each sign language has its own grammar, vocabulary, and cultural nuances, shaped by the communities that use them.

Just as French, Spanish, and Mandarin differ from one another, so do American Sign Language (ASL), British Sign Language (BSL), and Indian Sign Language (ISL).
